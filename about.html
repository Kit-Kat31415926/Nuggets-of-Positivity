About Nuggets of Positivity

The happiest place in the world

Nuggets of Positivity was created to encourage smiles and laughter! The mind is a deep ocean of thoughts, and oftentimes, people become entrenched in negative thoughts and self-deprecation unknowingly. This spiral into unhappiness spurred by the constant reminders of our shortcomings is detrimental to our overall well-being. With Nuggets of Positivity, people from all over are encouraged to share quick, uplifting statements to help brighten a stranger's day.
